Features

Our Featured Services

We are fast-growing analytics, machine learning and AI company committed to helping organizations take learned decisions by deriving insights from the vast amount of structured and unstructured data. We combine the deep skills in data science with the ability to develop high performance software to help organizations gain competitive edge.

Data Science

Our Data scientists understand business models and are great with data wrangling. But more importantly, they are trained to communicate well and present analytical models and their accompanying statistics in a business-friendly manner.

Data Engineering

To make analytics possible for businesses, we need a big data infrastructure, and it is our Data engineers who work in the background and help create that infrastructure. The Data engineers help identify the right Data platforms - relational databases, NoSQL, Hadoop, or Spark - depending on the business context.

Data Visualization

Our Data visualization engineers combine their ability of working with large volumes of data with Design elements so that the business personnel can leverage the full benefits of the Analytical model underneath. We build our solutions in Tableau, Spotfire, D3.JS and excel.

Data Psychology

Sometimes Businesses have a lot of raw data but may not really know what to do with it. Knowing how to put your data to work for you in the right way is essential to truly get to know more about your existing and potential customers.

Business Intelligence & Analytics

Our Data scientists understand business models and are great with data wrangling. But more importantly, they are trained to communicate well and present analytical models and their accompanying statistics in a business-friendly manner.

Software Development

Mobile or Web applications - We do a great job when you want your applications to not just sit there, but act on information coming to them without your intervention. We have built some cool augmented intelligence and artificial intelligence applications and will be happy to share our experience.
